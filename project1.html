UniFix — Predictive Maintenance

Project Type
Graduation Project

Date
2024 – 2025

Role
AI Researcher

UniFix is a predictive maintenance solution that facilities within the campus perimeter of Princess Nourah bint Abdulrahman University (PNU). More particularly, the objective is to create an intelligent prediction model to predict future failures of the device based on maintenance history from 2020 to the end of 2023.

The histories were examined to detect behavioral trends and patterns of failure in campus equipment and facilities. The project is an adaptive and very customizable predictive part, neither a stand-alone device nor a stand-alone system; it is an intelligent model that will be integrated into PNU’s current maintenance management system.

This design pattern makes the system scalable and flexible and pluggable into other platforms or expandable in the future if necessary.
