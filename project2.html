AI Story Generator
For Kids

Project Type
Website Including a Model

Date
2024

Role
AI/ML Engineer & Front and Backend Developer

The AI Story Generator for Kids is a smart and safe storytelling tool that combines the creativity of ChatGPT with a naughty-word classifier to ensure children receive only age-appropriate content.

Children (or parents/teachers) provide simple prompts, like characters, places, or situations — and the system generates an engaging, and imaginative story. The stories can be personalized to include the child's name, favorite animals, or fantasy elements like robots, unicorns, or time travel.

To ensure child safety and family friendliness, the generated text is automatically passed through a profanity and content filter — a classification model trained to detect and block inappropriate or unsafe words. This extra layer ensures that the content remains clean, appropriate, and suitable for kids of various age groups.
